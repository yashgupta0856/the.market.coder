Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP)

The Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP) is a structural continuation setup observed in strong uptrending stocks as volatility compresses and selling pressure diminishes near the right edge of a base. This contraction reflects institutional absorption of supply, where price tightens without requiring aggressive upside movement.
QuantFusion does not predict breakouts or price targets. Instead, it objectively classifies whether a stock satisfies the trend, volatility, price behavior, and volume conditions required before a breakout becomes possible. VCP is treated strictly as a market structure qualification, not a trading signal.
