Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP)

The Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP) is a structural continuation framework observed in strong uptrending stocks, where volatility progressively compresses and downside participation diminishes near the right edge of a base. This behavior reflects controlled institutional absorption rather than speculative demand.
QuantFusion does not forecast breakouts or define price targets. Instead, it evaluates whether a stock satisfies objective trend, volatility, price behavior, and volume conditions prior to any potential expansion. VCP is treated strictly as a structural qualification framework — not a trading signal.
